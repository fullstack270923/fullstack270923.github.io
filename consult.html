Medical Chat - Ask Your Questions
What are the symptoms of COVID-19?
COVID-19 symptoms include fever, dry cough, and fatigue. Some patients may also experience loss of taste or smell, body aches, and shortness of breath.
Should I take aspirin daily to prevent heart attacks?
Aspirin therapy should only be taken under the guidance of a doctor. It can help prevent heart attacks in some people, but it also increases the risk of bleeding.
How can I improve my immune system?
To improve your immune system, maintain a healthy diet, exercise regularly, get enough sleep, manage stress, and avoid smoking. Consult your doctor for personalized advice.
